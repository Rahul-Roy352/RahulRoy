Why Work With Bikalpbiswa Foundation?

At Bikalpbiswa Foundation, we believe that meaningful work comes from making a real difference in people's lives. When you join our team or volunteer with us, you become part of a community dedicated to sustainable development and positive change.

We offer a collaborative environment where your skills, passion, and ideas can have a direct impact on communities across Nepal. Whether you're looking for a career in development work or want to contribute your time as a volunteer, we provide opportunities for growth, learning, and meaningful contribution.

What We Offer

  • Purpose-driven work that creates real impact
  • Professional development opportunities and training
  • Collaborative environment with passionate colleagues
  • Flexible arrangements for work-life balance
  • Competitive benefits for full-time positions
  • Direct community engagement and field experience

Internship Programs

Gain valuable experience while contributing to meaningful projects

Student Internship Program

Our internship program offers students the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in development work while contributing to real projects that impact communities.

Program Details

  • Duration: 3-6 months (flexible based on academic schedule)
  • Areas: Program implementation, research, communications, finance, and administration
  • Benefits: Stipend, certificate, recommendation letter, networking opportunities
  • Requirements: Currently enrolled student, relevant field of study, commitment to social work

Application Timeline

  • Summer Internships: Apply by March 31st
  • Semester Internships: Rolling applications accepted
  • Research Internships: Year-round based on project needs
