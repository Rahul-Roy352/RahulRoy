Featured Campaign

Our current priority campaign making the biggest impact

Clean Water for All

Progress: 75% Complete | Target: $50,000 | Raised: $37,500

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet millions in rural Nepal still lack this basic necessity. Our Clean Water for All campaign aims to provide sustainable water solutions to 25 remote communities.

Campaign Goals

  • Install 25 water purification systems
  • Train 100 community water technicians
  • Provide clean water access to 10,000 people
  • Establish long-term maintenance protocols

Campaign Updates

Latest progress and success stories from our active campaigns

Clean Water Campaign Milestone

December 18, 2024 | Clean Water for All

Exciting update! We've successfully installed the 18th water purification system in Dolakha district. This new system will provide clean water access to over 400 people in the remote village of Suspa.

Impact: 18 systems installed, 7,200+ people now have clean water access

50 New Scholarships Awarded

December 15, 2024 | Education for All

We're thrilled to announce that 50 more deserving students have received scholarships through our Education for All campaign. These scholarships will support their education from grade 6 through high school completion.

Total Impact: 150 active scholarship recipients across 12 districts

Women's Cooperative Success Story

December 10, 2024 | Women Empowerment

The Janakpur Women's Cooperative, established through our empowerment program, has achieved remarkable success. The 25-member group has collectively saved $5,000 and started 15 small businesses.

Achievement: 95% of members increased their monthly income by 40% or more

Completed Campaigns

Successful campaigns that have made lasting impact in communities

School Building Initiative

100% Complete | Raised: $45,000

Successfully built 5 new schools in remote areas of Karnali Province, providing education access to over 800 children who previously had to walk hours to reach the nearest school.

Final Impact: 5 schools built, 800+ students enrolled, 25 teachers employed

COVID-19 Relief Campaign

100% Complete | Raised: $60,000

Provided emergency support during the COVID-19 pandemic, distributing food packages, masks, sanitizers, and medical supplies to over 2,000 vulnerable families.

Final Impact: 2,000+ families supported, 10,000+ masks distributed, 50+ isolation centers set up

Maternal Health Program

100% Complete | Raised: $35,000

Comprehensive maternal health program that trained 100 birth attendants and established 15 birthing centers in rural areas, dramatically reducing maternal and infant mortality.

Final Impact: 95% reduction in maternal mortality, 1,500+ safe deliveries, 100 trained attendants
