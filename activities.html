Health Care Programs

Improving health outcomes through accessible and quality healthcare services

Mobile Health Camps

Program Overview: Our mobile health camps bring essential medical services directly to remote and underserved communities where healthcare access is limited.

Services Provided:

  • General health checkups and consultations
  • Vaccinations and immunizations
  • Basic diagnostic services
  • Health education and awareness programs
  • Distribution of essential medicines
  • Maternal and child health services

Impact: Over 25,000 patients served through 150+ health camps across 45 districts.

Maternal & Child Health

Program Focus: Reducing maternal and infant mortality through comprehensive care and education programs.

Key Components:

  • Prenatal and postnatal care services
  • Safe delivery assistance and training
  • Nutrition education for mothers and children
  • Immunization programs for infants
  • Family planning counseling

Achievements: 95% reduction in maternal mortality in our target areas over the past 5 years.

Mental Health Awareness

Addressing mental health stigma through awareness campaigns, counseling services, and community support programs. We provide training for community health workers to identify and address mental health issues at the grassroots level.

Recent Activities:
  • Mental Health First Aid training for 200+ community leaders
  • Counseling services for 500+ individuals
  • Awareness campaigns reaching 10,000+ people

Education Initiatives

Empowering communities through quality education and skill development

Educational Workshops & Training

Workshop Categories:

  • Digital Literacy: Computer skills and internet literacy for all ages
  • Financial Literacy: Basic banking, savings, and financial planning
  • Health Education: Hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention
  • Vocational Training: Skills development for income generation
  • Leadership Development: Community leadership and governance training

Annual Impact: 50+ workshops conducted, 2,000+ participants trained across various programs.

Scholarship Program

Providing financial support to deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds to pursue higher education.

Current Support:

  • 500+ active scholarship recipients
  • Coverage from primary to university level
  • Additional mentoring and career guidance
  • 85% graduation success rate

School Infrastructure

Building and renovating school facilities to create conducive learning environments.

Infrastructure Projects:

  • 25 schools built or renovated
  • Library and computer lab setups
  • Safe drinking water and sanitation facilities
  • Solar power installations

Adult Literacy

Literacy programs for adults who missed formal education opportunities.

Program Features:

  • Flexible evening and weekend classes
  • Culturally appropriate curriculum
  • Focus on practical life skills
  • 1,200+ adults achieved literacy

Social Welfare Programs

Building stronger, more inclusive communities through targeted social programs

Women Empowerment

Comprehensive programs to empower women through education, skills training, and leadership development. Our initiatives focus on economic independence, health awareness, and social participation.

Key Components: Leadership training, microcredit programs, health education, legal awareness, and entrepreneurship support.

Child Protection

Safeguarding children's rights and ensuring their protection from abuse, neglect, and exploitation. We work to create child-friendly environments in communities.

Services: Child protection advocacy, safe spaces creation, awareness campaigns, and support for vulnerable children.

Disability Support

Inclusive programs ensuring people with disabilities have equal access to opportunities and can participate fully in community life.

Support Areas: Assistive devices, accessibility improvements, skills training, and advocacy for inclusive policies.

Poverty Alleviation Programs

Creating sustainable pathways out of poverty through economic empowerment

Microfinance & Credit

Small-scale financial services enabling community members to start or expand small businesses.

Program Impact:

  • 2,500+ active borrowers
  • 98% loan repayment rate
  • Average income increase of 40%
  • 1,800+ new businesses started

Skill Development

Vocational training programs providing marketable skills for sustainable employment and income generation.

Training Areas:

  • Tailoring and garment making
  • Carpentry and masonry
  • Agriculture and livestock
  • Small business management

Agriculture Support

Modern farming techniques, seeds, and tools to improve agricultural productivity and food security.

Support Provided:

  • High-yield seeds and fertilizers
  • Modern farming equipment
  • Irrigation system improvements
  • Market linkage facilitation
