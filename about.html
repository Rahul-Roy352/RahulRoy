Our SAATHI Story

Suport and association help ignore SAATHI was established in 2008 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create sustainable change in communities that need it most. What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a comprehensive organization reaching thousands of lives across odisha.

Our journey began when our founders witnessed the challenges faced by rural communities - limited access to healthcare, educational opportunities, and basic necessities. This sparked a commitment to bridge these gaps through innovative programs and community-centered approaches.

Over the years, we have evolved from a local initiative to a recognized foundation, maintaining our core values while expanding our impact. Today, we continue to work tirelessly to empower communities, believing that sustainable development comes from within when people are given the right tools and opportunities.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

The Saathi that drives everything we do

Our Mission

To empower communities through comprehensive programs in healthcare, education, social welfare, and economic development, creating sustainable positive change that improves quality of life for all.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to the resources, opportunities, and support needed to thrive, where poverty is eliminated, and where sustainable development benefits everyone.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical practices in all our operations
  • Compassion: Genuine care for the communities we serve
  • Innovation: Creative solutions to complex challenges
  • Sustainability: Long-term impact over short-term fixes
  • Collaboration: Working together with communities and partners

Our Approach

How we create sustainable impact in communities

Community-Centered

We believe that sustainable change comes from within communities. Our approach involves extensive consultation with community members to understand their needs, priorities, and cultural context before designing any program.

Sustainable Solutions

All our programs are designed for long-term impact. We focus on building local capacity, training community members, and creating systems that can continue to function independently.

Collaborative Partnerships

We work closely with local governments, other NGOs, healthcare institutions, and educational organizations to maximize our impact and avoid duplication of efforts.
